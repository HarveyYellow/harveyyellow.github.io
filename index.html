Biography

Hi, there! My name is Jingyuan Huang, and I am currently a PhD student specializing in Artificial Intelligence at UGA. At present, I am under the supervision of Prof. Ninghao Liu and Prof. Xiaoming Zhai, focusing on VLM.

Research Interests

My research interests primarily focus on the biases and robustness of large language models and vision-language models.
